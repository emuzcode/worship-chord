# Credits

This project includes the following public-domain (PD) hymns. All texts are confirmed PD based on the criteria below.

## Japanese public-domain hymns

Most Japanese church songs of the Meiji (1868–1912) and Taishō (1912–1926) eras were translated by missionaries and early Japanese Christians whose translators have been gone for more than 70 years, placing the translations in the public domain under Japanese copyright law (post-mortem auctoris + 70 years, TPP-aligned). These translations carry a quiet cultural weight: they are how Japanese churches first sang these hymns in their own language, and most of them have aged into the public domain just in the last decade.

## User-added songs

Users can add their own hymns through the in-browser UI on `/my`. These songs are stored locally in the user's browser (IndexedDB via Dexie.js) and are **never** transmitted to the server or included in this repository. Users are responsible for ensuring their own additions comply with applicable copyright law.

## Avoided sources

Hymns from modern Japanese hymnals (新生讃美歌 / 讃美歌21 / 教会福音讃美歌) are intentionally **not** included. These hymnals' translations are protected by editorial copyright and would require individual licensing.
