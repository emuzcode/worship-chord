# Credits

This project includes the following public-domain (PD) hymns as initial MVP content.
All texts are confirmed PD based on the criteria below.

## Included hymns (MVP, 5 songs)

| # | Title | Lyricist | Composer | Year | PD basis |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Amazing Grace | John Newton (1725–1807) | "New Britain" tune (traditional) | 1779 | Author died > 200 years ago |
| 2 | Be Thou My Vision | Mary Elizabeth Byrne (1880–1931, translator) + Irish traditional | "Slane" Irish traditional | medieval / 1905 | Translator died > 70 years ago + ancient Irish original |
| 3 | It Is Well with My Soul | Horatio Spafford (1828–1888) | Philip Bliss (1838–1876) | 1873 | US Copyright Office 1978 determination of PD |
| 4 | Holy, Holy, Holy! | Reginald Heber (1783–1826) | John B. Dykes (1823–1876) | 1826 / 1861 | Authors died > 150 years ago |
| 5 | Great Is Thy Faithfulness | Thomas Chisholm (1866–1960) | William Runyan (1870–1957) | 1923 | Entered US PD in 2019 (95-year rule) |

## User-added songs

Users can add their own hymns through the in-browser UI. These songs are stored locally in the user's browser (IndexedDB via Dexie.js) and are **never** transmitted to the server or included in this repository. Users are responsible for ensuring their own additions comply with applicable copyright law.
